Welcome to Hyrax Land!

The rock hyrax is a small animal native to rocky desert environments in Africa and the Middle East.

In this game you can explore their natural rocky habitat with a textured terrain of several rock formations!

Hyrax's are also well known for their "Awawa" screams, and so you can use the toggles above to control their "Awawa's"!

If you shift-click you'll disturb the Hyrax's and they'll all "Awawa" at you!

A fun fact about Hyrax's is that their closest living relatives are elephants and manatees! :D
